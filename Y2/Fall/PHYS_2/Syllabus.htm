  • Less than 60% of total points or less than 50% of exam points indicates a failing grade.

Q 1.9 refers to one of the 'Questions' at the back of the chapter
EP 1.6 refers to one of the 'Exercises' or 'Problems' at the end of the chapter
TU 1.2 refers to one of the 'Test Your Understanding'' questions at the end of each section in the chapter

Answers to TU questions are at the end of each chapter, after the problems, along with the answer to the Chapter Opening Question

Most chapters have a 'Bridging' question at the end; the answer to this question is also at the very end of the chapter.
Sections in parentheses will be discussed but not required for examinations.
